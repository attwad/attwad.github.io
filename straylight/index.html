Joining Straylight

I received a well-written and quite formal invitation to a human centric incubator led by two friends and former Google colleagues in Tokyo named Straylight.

It brings together a variety of persons with various skillsets to explore, learn an create new things at the intersection of technology, art and design.

I want to thank them for giving me the opportunity to join, I might be a tech person by day, I've always been an artist by night and heart so I hope I can bring something to the collective.
I will start coming regularly from September and hope to meet, think and make things with interesting folks gathered there.

let's make things!
